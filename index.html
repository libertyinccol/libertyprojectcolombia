About Us

Liberty Charter is an emerging charter aviation company headquartered in South Florida, strategically positioned to connect Colombia and Latin America through reliable and flexible air transportation services. Operating a growing fleet of U.S.-registered aircraft based in the Miami–Fort Lauderdale area, our mission is to provide executive charter, cargo, and specialized aviation solutions tailored to the region's increasing demand for efficient air connectivity. As we prepare to expand operations throughout Latin America, safety, professionalism, and customer service remain at the core of everything we do.
